Interesting Facts about Pandas

  1. Giant pandas are good at climbing trees and can also swim.
  2. An adult can eat 12–38 kilos of bamboo per day!
  3. Prehistoric pandas lived up to 2 million years ago.

Interesting Facts about Chameleons

  • Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the iguana suborder.
  • Changing skin color is an important part of communication among chameleons.
  • Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches.

Interesting Facts about Pigeons

  1. Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals.
  2. Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities.
  3. Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities.

Interesting Facts about Camels

  • There are two types of camels: One humped or “dromedary” camels and two humped Bactrian camels.
  • Camels have thick lips which let them forage for thorny plants other animals can’t eat.
  • Their humps let them store up to 80 pounds of fat which they can live off for weeks and even months!
